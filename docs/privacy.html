PRIVACY POLICY

Tap Tap App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising SDKs.

ON-DEVICE DATA

Your last 50 runs per mode are saved locally on your device so the History screen can show them. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it by removing the app's local data from your device settings.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app loads the “Press Start 2P” typeface from Google Fonts. Google may log the IP address of that request under its own privacy policy. No other data is sent.

CHILDREN

The app is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, children included.
